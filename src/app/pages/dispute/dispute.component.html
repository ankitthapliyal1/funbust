Dispute/Refund Policy for FunBust, an Indian Online Gaming App

Effective Date: 01 July 2023

At FunBust, we strive to provide a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience for our users. We understand that disputes and refund requests may arise from time to time. This Dispute/Refund Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for handling such situations. By using the FunBust mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the terms and practices described in this policy.

1. Disputes:

1.1. User Responsibility: If you encounter any issues or disputes related to the App, we encourage you to contact our customer support team as soon as possible. It is important to provide all relevant details and information regarding the dispute to help us investigate and address the matter efficiently.

1.2. Resolution Process: We will make reasonable efforts to resolve disputes promptly and in good faith. Our customer support team will review the dispute and communicate with you to gather additional information if necessary. We may require relevant screenshots, videos, or other evidence to assess the situation.

1.3. Fair Play and Anti-Cheating Policy: FunBust has a strict fair play and anti-cheating policy. If a dispute arises due to suspected cheating or violation of the App's terms and conditions, we may conduct a thorough investigation. If cheating or violation is confirmed, appropriate actions may be taken, including but not limited to account suspension or termination.

1.4. Binding Resolution: If we are unable to resolve a dispute through direct communication, either party may choose to pursue binding arbitration or seek legal remedies as provided by applicable laws and regulations.

2. Refunds:

2.1. Eligibility: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis and may be considered under specific circumstances. Refund requests must be submitted within a reasonable timeframe from the occurrence of the event that gives rise to the request.

2.2. Non-Refundable Items: Certain in-app purchases, virtual currencies, or virtual items acquired within the App may be non-refundable. This includes, but is not limited to, consumable items, upgrades, or items used in gameplay.

2.3. Grounds for Refunds: Refunds may be considered in cases of accidental purchases, unauthorized transactions, technical issues resulting in loss of in-game assets or virtual currency, or any other exceptional circumstances as determined by FunBust.

2.4. Refund Process: To request a refund, you must contact our customer support team and provide all relevant details regarding the transaction and the reason for the refund request. We will review the request and may require additional information or documentation to assess the validity of the claim.

2.5. Refund Method: Approved refunds will generally be issued in the same form as the original payment method. However, we reserve the right to determine the appropriate refund method on a case-by-case basis.

3.1. We reserve the right to modify or update this Dispute/Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on the App. Your continued use of the App after the modifications constitute your acceptance of the revised policy.

4. Contact Us:

4.1. If you have any questions or concerns about this Dispute/Refund Policy or need assistance with a dispute or refund request, please contact us at support@funbust.com.
